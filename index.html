About Us

Get to Know Us

Landcare NSW is a community-based organisation established in 2007 and based in Sydney. It was created for managing and protecting natural resources, in other words to look after our environment by creating sustainable farms, well cared for lands with a high level of biodiversity, and building more cohesive communities.

How We Operate

We operate through community groups throughout NSW that work with both government and non-government partners to participate in a wide range of impactful hands-on activities, workshops, community engagement and education.
